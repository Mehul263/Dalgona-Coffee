Dalgona Coffee Recipe Page

Dalgona coffee is a South Korean Coffee recipe which became the frontline news during the lockdown. This might fascinate everyone since all the ingredients needed are in your cupboard

Ingredients

  • Hot water
  • Instant coffee or expresso granules
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Making Process

Step 1: Combine the instant coffee, sugar and hot water
Add 2 tablespoons of instant coffee, 2 tablespooons of sugar and 1 cup of hot water to a mixing bowl


Step 2: Whisk, whisk and whisk until the coffee mixture is light and fluffy
Using an electric hand held mixer, whip the coffe is light and dusky brown in colour and fluffy


Step 3: Serve with milk
Use a spoon to garnish a glass of hot or cold milk with the coffee cream. Serve with a straw and stir, stir and stir to enjoy
